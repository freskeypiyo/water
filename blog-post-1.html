Why pH Levels Matter in Water

Author: Arpit Mishra | Date: October 12, 2023

Water is the essence of life, but not all water is created equal. One of the most critical factors determining the quality of your drinking water is its pH level. At FreskeyPiyo, we ensure our water maintains an optimal balance to support your body's natural functions.

What is pH?

pH stands for "potential of hydrogen." It is a measure of how acidic or basic water is. The range goes from 0 to 14, with 7 being neutral. Water with a pH less than 7 indicates acidity, whereas a pH greater than 7 indicates a base (alkaline).

The Benefits of Balanced Water

  • Improved Digestion: Proper pH levels help in neutralizing excess stomach acid.
  • Better Hydration: Some studies suggest alkaline water is absorbed more effectively by cells.
  • Detoxification: It assists the kidneys in filtering out toxins.
