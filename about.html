The Genesis of FreskeyPiyo


It all started with a simple observation. In the bustling markets of Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand, hydration is essential. But the choices were stagnant. All bottles looked the same.


Arpit Mishra and Daksh Aggarwal surveyed the market and realized two things:

  1. Reputable brands were too rigid. They didn't offer customization.
  2. Local brands compromised on quality and packaging design.
