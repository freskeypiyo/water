Commitment to Plastic Waste Management

Registration No: FRES/CPCB/EPR/2024-25/098

Freskey is officially registered with the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB). We strictly adhere to PWM (Plastic Waste Management) Rules 2016 (as amended) to ensure the collection, segregation, and recycling of post-consumer plastic waste.

Target: 100% Recovery

We work with authorized PROs (Producer Responsibility Organizations) to collect back equivalent weight of plastic we put into the market.

Circular Economy

Our bottles are sent to certified recycling units to be converted into flakes, fiber, and utility products, closing the loop.
