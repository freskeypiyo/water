Sustainability Mission

Why Square?

Square bottles fit perfectly next to each other without wasted space (gaps) that round bottles create. This means 20% more water per truck load, significantly reducing transportation emissions.


One Side Label

By restricting our labeling to a single side and printing directly on high-quality recyclable paper, we reduce chemical glue and plastic wrap usage, making the recycling process faster and cheaper.
