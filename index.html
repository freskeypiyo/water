Natural Filtration

Freskey’s water originates from protected underground borewell sources, where it is naturally collected over time through geological layers of soil and rock. These layers act as a natural filtration system, contributing to the clarity and neutral taste of the water.

Structured Responsibility

At Freskey, our responsibility begins after sourcing — to preserve the natural characteristics of the water while ensuring it meets all safety, hygiene, and regulatory requirements for packaged drinking water in India.

Quality Framework

Quality Is a System, Not a Claim

Quality at Freskey is built into processes, not slogans. Our checks are conducted at multiple stages to ensure safety and consistency.

1. Source Level

  • Regular source water testing
  • Monitoring physical parameters
  • Monitoring chemical parameters

2. Processing

  • Filtration & disinfection protocols
  • Compliance with applicable norms
  • Controlled handling environments

3. Bottling

  • Hygienic bottling environment
  • Automated/Semi-automated filling
  • Secure sealing & batch coding

4. Post-Production

  • Visual inspections
  • Storage under controlled conditions
  • Safe logistics handling


Result: Clean, clear, and dependable drinking water suitable for daily use.
