Green Pledge

Sustainability is our Second Nature

At Freskey, we understand the plastic problem. That is why we are committed to an EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) framework that ensures we collect back as much plastic as we distribute.

1

Collect

We work with waste management agencies to collect used PET bottles from the environment.

2

Crush

Collected bottles are sorted, cleaned, and crushed into flakes at certified recycling units.

3

Create

These flakes are converted into yarn for t-shirts, carpets, or other utility plastic items.
